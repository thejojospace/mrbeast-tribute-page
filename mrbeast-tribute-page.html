Timeline of Mr Beast's Life

  • 1998 - Born in Kansas, United States of America
  • 2012 - Starts the MrBeast channel on YouTube and uploads his first video
  • 2015 - Starts his "Worst Intros" series gaining initial traction to his channel
  • 2016 - Graduates from Greenville Christian Academy, North Carolina
  • 2017 - Uploads his first viral video of him counting to 100,000 taking 40 hours to complete, gaining 27 million views
  • 2018 - Participates in the PewDiePie vs T-Series rivalry, gain more attention to his channel, which culminates in a viral video while attending Super Bowl LIII
  • 2019 - Starts the Team Trees campaign to plant 20 million trees which is reached in only 2 months
  • 2021 - Releases his recreation of Squiad Games that obtains 325 million views
  • 2022 - Surpasses 100 million subsrcibers on his main channel
